Dr David Cashman is an educator, researcher, musician, and advocate for the music industry. As an educator, he is
passionate about preparing students for the unconventional rigours and challenges of the creative industries. As a
researcher, his area of research interest is live music performance and industry particularly in regional areas, in
tourism, and the performance practice thereof. He has worked with and consulted for universities, peak industry
bodies and local and state governments.
