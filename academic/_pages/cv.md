## Professional Summary

<div style="margin-left: 20px;">
Dr. David Cashman is a multifaceted professional, seamlessly blending his roles as an educator, researcher, musician, and music industry advocate. With a deep passion for education, he is committed to equipping students with the unique skills and knowledge required to thrive in the creative sectors. His research pursuits are anchored in the nuances of live music performance and its associated industries, with a special focus on regional spaces, tourism, and performance practices. Over the years, Dr. Cashman's expertise has been sought by universities, industry groups, and government entities at both local and state levels.
</div>
